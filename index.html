Frequently asked questions

Have any question? we are here to help

What is a Payment Gateway?

A payment gateway securely processes online transactions by encrypting sensitive details and routing them between merchants, banks and payment networks.

Which payment gateway is best for my business in India?

The best payment gateway depends on your business model, payment mode coverage, compliance needs and integration requirements. Exo Pay is a regulated payment gateway provider in India offering secure online and offline collections.

What payment options does Exo Pay support?

UPI, cards, net banking, wallets, Bharat QR, NACH and offline cash/cheque deposits through authorised counters.

How does Exo Pay support settlements?

Settlements are processed as per RBI guidelines, with predictable settlement cycles based on your payment mode usage.

Can I accept payments without a website?

Yes. You can create and share secure online payment links to collect payments without needing a website or app.

How secure is Exo Pay's payment gateway?

Exo Pay uses encrypted processing, fraud monitoring and robust infrastructure, and is RBI-authorised, PCI-DSS and ISO certified.

How do I integrate Exo Pay into my website or app?

Developers can integrate using APIs, SDKs and plug-ins—supported by detailed documentation and onboarding assistance.

Does Exo Pay provide offline payment collection?

Yes. Exo Pay enables hybrid online + offline collections through 1.4M+ authorised cash counters across India.

How does Exo Pay support businesses across India?

Exo Pay serves enterprises, SMEs and institutions across India through unified dashboards, compliance support and multi-mode collections.
